About

Nick is a soon to be graduate from the University of Illinois at Chicago's DPT program. He graduated from the University of Michigan with a degree in Industrial and Operations Engineering. Upon graduation, Nick joined ZS Associates, a management consulting firm, where he helped healthcare companies solve complex problems through data analytics.

While working in the corporate world, Nick realized he wanted to help patients directly, specifically with rehabilitation of musculoskeletal issues and injury reduction. Given his interests in basketball, weightlifting, and general strength and conditioning, Nick hopes to pursue a career in sports physical therapy.
